About Us

Heroes Match 3 Games is developed by Criss Cross Games, a studio passionate about merging fantasy RPG elements with puzzle-based mechanics. In this title, players step into the role of an evolving hero—starting as an elf warrior and rising through challenges to conquer orcs, monsters, and legendary beasts.
What sets us apart? Every match-3 puzzle is an interactive battle. Tactical decision-making, clever rune combinations, and character customization are at the core of the gameplay. We wanted to create not just a puzzle game, but a heroic journey powered by your matching skills.

How to Play

  • Match to Attack:

    Match 3 or more magic runes of the same type (fire, water, nature, sword) to perform an attack. The longer the match, the stronger the strike.

  • Select Your Hero's Move:

    Match 3 for a standard hit
    Match 4-5 for power spells or area attacks
    Match 6 for an ultimate move

  • Defend and Counter:

    Enemies don’t wait. Use defensive runes to shield or recover. The timing of your moves can be the difference between victory and defeat.

  • Upgrade Your Hero:

    After each battle, earn rewards to upgrade your sword, learn new spells, and improve health or mana stats. Customize your hero’s abilities to suit your style.

FAQ

  • Q1 What happens when I match more than three runes?
    A1 Matching 4-5 runes triggers powerful abilities like elemental spells, healing, or splash damage. Match 6 to activate the hero's ultimate skill.
  • Q2 Can I choose different characters?
    A2 Yes! You start as an elf warrior, but more heroes with unique powers unlock as you progress.
  • Q3 Are there boss battles in the game?
    A3 Definitely. You’ll face elite enemies that require strategy and timing to defeat. Prepare for a fight!
  • Q4 Do I need to be online to play?
    A4 No. Heroes Match 3 is fully playable offline. Online mode gives access to leaderboards and occasional events.
  • Q5 How can I earn upgrades faster?
    A5 Clear levels with higher combos and complete side quests for bonus rewards. Special challenges and daily login bonuses also help accelerate your hero’s growth.
