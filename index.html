AbOuT Us

  • Firesky brings a fresh approach to the classic match-3 genre with Match Stars. Our goal was to make each puzzle feel alive by giving players more control — not just through matching skills, but by choosing a Star companion that changes how they play.
  • Each character has a different style — some clear entire rows, others add power-ups, and a few offer game-saving maneuvers when you're stuck. Combined with our rich level designs and seamless offline experience, Match Stars offers casual gamers a deeper, more personal connection to the match-3 puzzle universe.
